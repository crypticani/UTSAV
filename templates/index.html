About Utsav


Utsav is the annual sports and cultural festival of Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya, Haridwar. It is organized every year in the month of February and March. At Utsav, students from all departments of DSVV participate to showcase their talent and passion for sports and cultural activities.
Get a glimpse of glory and energy of Utsav 2020 with this documentary. Embrace yourself and watch the highlights of the events that took place last season. From Basketball to Cricket; from dance to rangoli; discover all the beautiful and glorious memories and fill your hearts with curiosity.
